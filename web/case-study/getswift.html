About the work
Stage Fund, an investment firm, recently purchased GetSwift’s Product Suite, a last mile solutions platform.

They were looking for product and technology strategy across GetSwift’s product suite.

‍Through stakeholder/customer interviews and qualitative & market research, Double Nines helped Stage Fund prioritize the business units and their respective features.

Double Nines also provided a way forward to consolidating the businesses with a product roadmap as well as provided a variety of Go-To Market Strategies to activate new ways forward.
